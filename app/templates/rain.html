Just how wet is Ireland, really?

This a heatmap of the average monthly total at six Irish weather stations over the past sixty-odd years - Belmullet in the west, Valentia in the south, Malin Head in the north, Mullingar in the midlands, and the two airports in the Republic, Shannon and Dublin.

Ireland is middling wet, alright.
